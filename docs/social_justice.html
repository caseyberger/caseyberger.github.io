Resources for Equity, Inclusion, and Justice

This list of resources is by no means comprehensive and is a work in progress.

Antiracism

Being an ally is a lifelong process. The work never ends. Fortunately, there are many resources! These books are great places to start your antiracist research.

This list is always in progress – if you know of a great resource that is not on here, email me and tell me about it!

Feminism

Women (and non-binary folx) still do not have equal rights under the US Constitution (Don’t believe me? You can read all about it here). Womxn are underrepresented in institutions of power, from Congress to corporate leadership to academic institutions.
